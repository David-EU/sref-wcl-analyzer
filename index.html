Sref's Resto Druid Analyzer

Usage

Find a WarcraftLogs report you'd like to analyze, copy the URL (or just the report code) into the input box, and press the 'Get' button.

Select the specific fight you'd like to analyze, and press the 'Analyze' button. Analysis may take up to a minute to complete.

Analysis Info

This analyzer calculates how much of your healing can be attributed to each of your HoT's mastery stacks. For example, if you took the talent Cultivation, healing from *other* HoTs on a target with your Cultivation on it will heal for more because of the extra mastery stack provided by Cultivation. This is healing that wouldn't have happened had you not specced Cultivation, but it's completely invisible in basic logs. This analyzer seeks to remedy this issue.

Mastery stack analyzers are provided for all your HoTs, though you may find the most interesting to be the talented HoTs: Cultivation, Cenarion Ward, and Spring Blossoms. Oh, and yes: Frenzied Regeneration does count towards mastery for whatever reason.
